BriefProposalSummary

I grew up in a single parent home in the Logan Fontenelle Projects near 20th and Paul Streets In Omaha, NE in the 1980's and 1990's prior to being displaced as the area was repurposed. I now own and live home in this neighborhood at 2010 N. 20th St. I also personally own a single-family rental home adjacent to this proposed construction (2403 Florence Blvd). I formed a Nebraska LLC and purchased this vacant lot located at 2407 Florence Blvd on March 4, 2022. I want to provide affordable housing to others who are living the life that I know so well and to inspire one person or many to rise higher and to believe that they can achieve the American Dream. And I fervently want to proactively help to create a safer environment in a safer, well-kept neighborhood, for the children during their formative years and for a single parent or for a married couple as they establish a toehold and move toward financial stability. I want this to be the first of a series of similar construction and contribution.

Timeline

The real estate lot was purchased on March 4, 2022. The property has been cleaned up in the time since. I have met with a builder and have a project budget that will need to be updated (because of the volatility in construction material costs)after my request is approved.

ProposalDescriptionAndNeedsAlignment

There is an advantage, I think, for someone living in the neighborhood to have a greater positive impact on the community then from someone as an absentee property landlord or owner.

VisioningWorkshopFindingsAlignment

My experiences as a youth will allow me to communicate more effectively with the current members of this neighborhood and community and I think will allow me to be an active mentor to others. Promoting a 'Let's work together" atmosphere will contribute to a stronger and safer neighborhood. The increase in the number of affordable housing opportunities will help provide the workforce that will be needed as more businesses open in the targeted area 6 to 10 blocks east of my construction.

PrioritiesAlignment

My construction project is located within a qualified census tract and my involvement is directed toward helping to make this area a sustainable community. It provides needed housing and it provides a workforce for adjacent business growth.

EconomicImpact

The job creation is two fold. Immediately it will be for a contractor to build my duplex project. Longer term it will be to provide additional people living in the area who can fill future job needs in the downtown or redevelopment Makerhood area.

There are not any direct permanent jobs being created. The additional housing does help to provide the workforce supply needed in the adjacent area businesses.

EconomicImpactTemporaryJobsCreated

The temporary construction jobs will include a general contract and one or more people in each of the trade areas involved in the construction project, i.e. plumbing, electrical, carpentry, bricklayers etc.

CommunityBenefit

It will first and foremost be safe and secure living conditions from someone who can daily monitor and positively impact this community and by someone who himself lives in the neighborhood. My background and experience allows me to interact with both the adult resident(s) and the child resident(s) in a way that I can encourage them to develop their own skills or education and seek to rise higher.

CommunityBenefitSustainability

And being active in the community will promote a safer environment, especially for the children, so they might have greater upward mobility potential. I can and will make a difference.
