BriefProposalSummary

YBusiness Solutions LLC is currently formed as a Limited Liability Company under Nebraska Sate laws on May 3, 2019. YBusiness Solutions LLC provides mentoring for small business owners, and technical management training for automated customized workflow management systems. This system is designed by our team and is fully customizable for clients who need Secure Portal Access, Marketing System, CRM Management System, File Sharing & Exchange, Calendar & Scheduling, Billing & Subscriptions, Managing Projects and Tasks, Managing Teams, White Label Branding & Customization, Contracts & E-signatures. YBusiness Solutions Training Academy is the platform build to train, both youth and seniors who are looking to start a business, manage a business or need personal development skills. Programs also include cybersecurity, understanding taxes, financial fundamentals, and other classes as needed.
